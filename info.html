Focus. Financial applications love throwing the kitchen sink at you. We do one thing and one thing well: make it easy to track your debts, and figure out the fastest possible way to pay them off. That's all you need! So we make it a priority to ensure you get that.
Security. Other systems often require a connection to all of your banking institutions, and in some cases, those banks ask for additional fees, just to connect!. This is about paying off debt, not accruing more! With us, no bank account connections are required, and no credit card numbers stored. Under the hood, it's just a list of balances with no connection to your personal info.
No scary stuff. No machine learning. No AI. No blockchain. No bitcoin. No hooking up to your bank and charging you hidden fees. No secret surveillance listening in over your phone. Just a simple app doing basic math for you -- math based entirely on the world's finest economic and money manager minds. This isn't rocket surgery, so there won't be any rise of the machines on our watch! To those buzzwords we say...no thanks!
Your data is yours and nobody else's. We value your freedom to choose a competitor. If you don't like us, or find something better, we give you a single button to access all your data. That's it! It's yours!...as it should be. We'd be sorry to lose you, but we care more about your success than our own.
Support. Paying off debt is hard. It requires discipline, patience, and tenacity over a long period of time. Everyone's financial situation is different and it is tough to talk about. We understand that. Ours is a community of shared ideals. No one should be excluded from help or support because of a lack of funds. This is why our base functionality is 100% free, support is one click away, and we provide the means for others to donate/gift subscriptions to our users.
